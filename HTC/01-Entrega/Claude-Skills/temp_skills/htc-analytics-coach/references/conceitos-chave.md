# Conceitos-Chave: Analytics para High Ticket

## 1. Filosofia: Data Over Opinions

What gets measured gets managed. High Ticket businesses succeed making decisions based on data.

**Hierarchy:**
1. Vanity Metrics (feel good, don't drive decisions)
2. Activity Metrics (show effort, don't guarantee results)
3. Output Metrics (show production, link to results)
4. Outcome Metrics (show business health, drive decisions) ‚Üê FOCUS HERE

## 2. Essential High Ticket Metrics

### Front-End (Lead Generation)
- Total visitors by source
- Lead capture rate (target: 30-50%)
- Cost per lead (CPL)

### Mid-End (Nurturing)
- Email open rate (target: 25-40%)
- Click rate (target: 3-8%)
- Webinar show-up rate (target: 20-40%)
- Attendee-to-buyer rate (target: 5-15%)

### Back-End (Sales)
- Lead-to-customer rate (target: 3-10%)
- Call show-up rate (target: 70-90%)
- Call conversion rate (target: 20-40%)
- Revenue per lead, per customer

### Financial
- CAC (target: <33% of first customer value)
- LTV
- LTV:CAC ratio (target: >3:1)
- Payback period (target: <3 months)
- ROAS (target: 3-5x minimum)
- Gross margin (target: >70% for digital)

## 3. Dashboard Templates

### Weekly Dashboard
- Traffic & leads
- Nurturing metrics
- Sales (calls, revenue, conversion)
- Quick calculations

### Monthly Dashboard
- All weekly metrics averaged
- Channel performance comparison
- Content performance ranking
- Year-over-year comparison
- Goal progress

## 4. Analysis Frameworks

### The "So What?" Test
For every metric: "So what? What do I DO with this?"

### The 5-Why Analysis
When metric is off, ask "Why?" 5 times to find root cause.

### The Correlation Analysis
Look for relationships: "When email open rate >30%, sales increase 40%"

## 5. Optimization Strategies

### Low Conversion Rate (<3%)
- Audit lead quality
- Improve lead magnet relevance
- Strengthen email nurture
- Add social proof
- Test different offers

### High Cost Per Lead
- Improve ad creative
- Refine targeting
- Test different lead magnets
- Improve landing page
- Try different platforms

### Low Email Open Rate (<25%)
- Improve subject lines
- Clean email list
- Test send times
- Segment better

## 6. A/B Testing Framework

### Priority:
1. Headlines (biggest impact)
2. Lead magnets
3. Offer structure
4. Pricing
5. CTA buttons

### Process:
1. Identify problem metric
2. Form hypothesis
3. Create variant (one change)
4. Split traffic 50/50
5. Run to 95% significance
6. Implement winner
7. Document learning
