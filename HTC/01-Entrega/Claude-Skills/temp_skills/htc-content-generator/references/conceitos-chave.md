# Conceitos-Chave: Content Generation para High Ticket

## 1. Filosofia: Value-First Content

80/20 Rule:
- 80% of content: Pure value, education, inspiration
- 20% of content: Selling, promotion, offers

## 2. Content Pillars

### Pillar 1: Education (How-To)
- Step-by-step tutorials
- Frameworks and systems
- Tips and tricks
- Common mistakes to avoid
- Quick wins

### Pillar 2: Inspiration (Transformation)
- Before/after stories
- Client transformations
- Personal journey stories
- Vision and future-casting

### Pillar 3: Connection (Personal)
- Behind-the-scenes
- Personal stories
- Values and beliefs
- Vulnerable moments

### Pillar 4: Social Proof (Results)
- Testimonials
- Case studies
- Success metrics
- Reviews and feedback

### Pillar 5: Promotion (Offers)
- Program announcements
- Special offers
- Enrollment reminders
- Event promotions

## 3. Platform-Specific Strategies

### LinkedIn (B2B)
- Post 4-5x/week
- Mix: 40% educational, 30% stories, 20% cases, 10% promo
- Formats: Text, image, video, document
- Engage 15-30 min/day on prospects' posts

### Instagram (B2C)
- Feed: 3-4x/week, Stories daily, Reels 2-3x/week
- Content: Educational carousels, testimonials, behind-the-scenes
- Hashtags: 5-10 niche + 3-5 branded

### Email Newsletter
- 1-2x/week minimum
- Structure: Personal intro + Value section + Client spotlight + Soft CTA
- Mobile-first design, one clear CTA

### YouTube
- 1-2x/week
- Types: How-to, case studies, interviews, behind-the-scenes
- Video: Hook (0-30s) → Intro (30s-2min) → Content (2-15min) → CTA

## 4. Content Calendar Template

### Weekly Rhythm
- Monday: Educational post
- Wednesday: Value/how-to
- Friday: Proof/case study
- Ongoing: Stories/engagement

### Monthly Rhythm
- Week 1: Theme introduction
- Week 2: Deep dive/education
- Week 3: Implementation/examples
- Week 4: Case study/results

## 5. Content Repurposing

1 Core Piece → 10+ Pieces:
- YouTube 20min → 5 social posts, 3 stories, 2 Reels, 1 email, 1 LinkedIn article, 1 infographic
