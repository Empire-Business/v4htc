## METRICS TO TRACK

### Front-End:
- Landing page visitors
- Opt-in rate (target: 30-50%)
- Cost per lead (CPL)

### Middle:
- Email open rate (target: 25-40%)
- Email click rate (target: 3-8%)
- VSL views (if tracking)
- VSL completion rate (target: 40-70%)

### Back-End:
- Sales page visits
- Conversion rate (target: 2-8%)
- Cost per acquisition (CPA)
- Return on ad spend (ROAS)
