# Conceitos-Chave: Estratégia de Marketing para High Ticket

## 1. Filosofia: Qualidade > Quantidade

High Ticket marketing difere de low-ticket:
- Lead quality > Lead quantity
- Trust-building é essencial
- Relationship-focused, não transaction-focused
- Metrics que matter: CAC, LTV, ROAS (não vanity metrics)

## 2. Framework de Planejamento Estratégico

### Fase 1: Foundation (Semana 1-2)
1. Defina ICA (Ideal Client Avatar)
2. Clarifique UVP (Unique Value Proposition)
3. Mapeie Customer Journey

### Fase 2: Channel Strategy (Semana 3-4)
- Top of Funnel: Awareness (orgânico, paid, content)
- Middle: Nurture (email, retargeting, webinars)
- Bottom: Conversion (calls, direct outreach)

### Fase 3: Content Strategy (Mês 2)
- 4 Pillars: Problem Education, Solution Intro, Proof & Results, Implementation Tips
- Weekly e Monthly rhythms

### Fase 4: Lead Generation (Mês 3)
- 4 Engines: Content, Referral, Paid Traffic, Partnerships

### Fase 5: Metrics & Optimization (Ongoing)
- KPIs por estágio do funil
- Dashboard templates

## 3. Canais por Offer Type

**B2B Consulting/Coaching:**
- LinkedIn (organic + outreach)
- Podcast guesting
- FB/LinkedIn Ads
- Email marketing

**B2C Health/Fitness:**
- Instagram/TikTok (organic)
- Meta Ads
- YouTube
- Influencer partnerships

**Digital Courses:**
- SEO blog + YouTube
- Webinars
- Affiliate partnerships
- Meta/Google Ads

## 4. Budget Allocation

**Start-up (Mês 1-3):** 50% conteúdo, 30% teste, 20% tools
**Growth (Mês 4-12):** 40% ads, 30% conteúdo, 20% teste, 10% tools
**Scale (Ano 1+):** 50% ads, 25% conteúdo, 15% team, 10% innovation

**Regra:** Monthly marketing budget = 10-20% of target revenue
**CAC target:** <33% of first customer value
**LTV:CAC target:** >3:1

## 5. Métricas Chave

**Awareness:**
- Content reach, website traffic, social growth

**Lead Gen:**
- Lead volume, quality scores, CPL, conversion rate

**Nurturing:**
- Email open/click rates, webinar metrics, engagement

**Conversion:**
- Lead-to-customer rate, call conversion, CPA, LTV, ROAS

**Financial:**
- CAC, LTV, Payback Period, Margins

## 6. Estratégia de Campanha

**Pre-Launch (2 semanas):** Teaser, waitlist, testing
**Launch Week (1 semana):** Daily content, lives, urgency, ads
**Post-Launch (1-2 semanas):** Retargeting, case studies, analysis
