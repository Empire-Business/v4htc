## Use este script para calls de 30-60 minutos

**Research (15 min antes):**
- Check social media/profile
- Review application responses
- Prepare specific questions
- Review their pain points

**Mindset Set:**
- I'm here to help, not sell
- I'm looking for fit, not forcing

## CALL SCRIPT (30-60 min)

### 0-5 min: Rapport & Context

**You:** "Thanks for making time today, [Name]. I've been looking forward to our conversation."

**Pause for response**

**You:** "Before we dive in, I want to make sure I respect your time. We've got 45-60 minutes together - does that still work for you?"

**[Wait for confirmation]**

**You:** "Perfect. Here's what I'd propose for our time together:

1. First, I'd like to learn about your situation - where you are, what you've tried, what's working and not working

2. Then I'll share what might help based on my experience working with [number] of [similar clients]

3. Finally, we can decide if there's a fit to work together

Sound fair?"

**[Get agreement]**

**You:** "Great! Also, just so you know - I only work with people I genuinely believe I can help. If at the end it doesn't feel like the right fit, I'll tell you. I don't want to waste your time or mine. Fair?"

---

### 5-25 min: Discovery (The Deep Dive)

**You:** "Let's start with your situation. Can you tell me a bit about [current situation]?"

**[Listen, take notes]**

**You:** "How long have you been dealing with [challenge]?"

**[Listen]**

**You:** "What have you tried so far to solve this?"

**[Listen - make note of what they've attempted]**

**You:** "And what were the results with those approaches?"

**[Listen - understanding what hasn't worked]**

**Transition to Pain:**

**You:** "I want to understand better - what's this [challenge] costing you? Not just financially, but in terms of [time/stress/relationships/health]?"

**[Listen for emotional impact]**

**You:** "What happens if this doesn't get solved in the next 6-12 months? Where do you see yourself?"

**[Listen - paint the picture of staying stuck]**

**Transition to Desire:**

**You:** "On the flip side, if you could solve this - what would your ideal situation look like? Be specific."

**[Listen - understand their vision]**

**You:** "What would [result] mean for you? For your [family/team/business]?"

**[Listen - emotional connection]**

**Budget Revelation:**

**You:** "I want to make sure I present the right solution for you. Investment-wise, what have you budgeted for solving this challenge? Or what feels comfortable to you?"

**[If they say they don't know]**

**You:** "Totally understand. What I typically see is people investing anywhere from R$[range] to solve this kind of challenge. Does that feel like it's in the ballpark, or way off?"

**Decision Process:**

**You:** "Besides yourself, who else would be involved in making a decision like this?"

**[Listen]**

**You:** "How do you typically make decisions? Do you like to sleep on things, or when you see the right fit, do you move forward?"

---

### 25-35 min: The Pivot & Presentation

**You:** "Thanks for sharing all of that. Let me make sure I've got this right...

[Paraphrase back their situation]

So you're dealing with [main challenge] and you've tried [what they tried] but haven't been able to [what they want]. And the cost of staying where you is [reiterate pain point]. Is that accurate?"

**[Wait for confirmation]**

**You:** "Great. Here's what I know:

[Name], I've helped [number] of [people in similar situation] with exactly this challenge. And I have good news: This is solvable.

Here's how we'd approach it..."

**[Present your solution - customized to what they shared]**

**You:** "Now, you might be wondering about the investment. This program is R$[price], and I know that can feel like a lot.

But let me ask you: Based on what we've discussed, if this helps you [specific outcome], what would that be worth to you?"

**[Wait - let them answer]**

**You:** "And when you think about it that way - compared to the cost of staying where you are - does the investment feel more reasonable?"

**[Wait]**

---

### 35-50 min: Closing

**You:** "So, [Name], based on everything we've discussed - your situation, your goals, your timeline - does this feel like the right fit for you?"

**[If YES]**

**You:** "Excellent! I'm excited to work with you. Here's what happens next:

[Next steps - payment, onboarding, start date]

Does that work for you?"

**[If MAYBE/HESITANT]**

**You:** "Fair enough. What would help you feel more confident?"

**[Address concern]**

**You:** "Does that help?"

**[If still hesitant]**

**You:** "I understand. Here's what I'd suggest: Why don't you sleep on it, and I'll send you a summary of everything we discussed. Would tomorrow at [time] work for a quick follow-up call?"

**[If NO]**

**You:** "Totally fine. Can I ask what doesn't feel right?"

**[Listen]**

**You:** "I appreciate your honesty. This might not be the right fit, and that's okay. Would you mind if I stayed in touch with relevant content? No pressure."

---

### 50-60 min: Next Steps (Depending on Outcome)

**If they bought:**
- Confirm payment details
- Set expectations
- Express excitement
- Send immediate follow-up email

**If they're thinking:**
- Send summary email
- Schedule follow-up
- Add to nurture sequence

**If they said no:**
- Thank them
- Ask for feedback
- Keep door open
- Add to general list
