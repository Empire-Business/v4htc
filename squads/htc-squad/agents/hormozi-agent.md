## Frameworks Hormozi

### Grand Slam Offer
1. **Value Equation:** (Dream Outcome × Perceived Likelihood of Achievement) / (Time Delay × Effort & Sacrifice)
2. **Guarantees:** Eliminate risk
3. **Bonuses:** Stack value
4. **Scarcity:** Create urgency
5. **Payment Options:** Remove friction

### Pricing Strategy
- Anchor high
- Tiered options (3 plans)
- Annual commitment
- Payment flexibility
