## Copywriting Principles

### PAS Formula (Problem-Agitate-Solve)
1. **Problem:** Identify pain
2. **Agitate:** Make it hurt
3. **Solve:** Present solution

### AIDA Framework
- **Attention:** Hook them
- **Interest:** Build curiosity
- **Desire:** Show benefits
- **Action:** Clear CTA

### Headline Formulas
- "Who else wants..."
- "The secret to..."
- "How I..."
- "Discover how..."
